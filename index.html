// The Origin

At 7, I took apart my mother’s laptop and repurposed the monitor into a working TV — no instructions, no help, just pattern recognition and the need to make it work.

At 11, I took a broken scooter, reverse-engineered its motor into a generator, and wired it to charge the battery that powered it — with a working emergency light attached. No kit. No guide. Pure systems thinking.

That instinct has never left. It just scaled.

I’m Mikal Bright, Sr., founder of Bright Future Technologies LLC and the engineer behind BFT Services, an AI-powered operations management platform built for logistics, dispatch, and triage workflows.

With 13+ years of enterprise IT experience, I help organizations modernize infrastructure, automate operations, strengthen security, and turn complex technology into practical business outcomes. My background spans Azure cloud engineering, enterprise automation, PowerShell development, Azure Virtual Desktop, identity governance, AI agents, Power Platform solutions, and custom software development.

Bright Future Technologies was built on one principle: technology should reduce friction, not create more of it. Whether the engagement is cloud modernization, AI strategy, automation, drone media, website development, or smart home technology, the goal is the same: build systems that are clear, scalable, useful, and built to last.
