About Us

Hi, I'm Mikal Bright, Sr., owner of Bright Future Technologies LLC. With over 12 years of IT experience, I founded this company to bring professional-grade technology solutions to individuals, families, and small businesses in the Atlanta area.

We specialize in making technology practical and accessible — whether that's a custom-built PC tailored to your workflow, a smart home system that simplifies daily life, or breathtaking 4K aerial footage of your property or event.

As a licensed FAA drone pilot and photographer, I capture aerial video and photography that would otherwise require expensive plane or helicopter rentals. Every project is approached with a commitment to quality, customization, and your complete satisfaction. I'm also a Microsoft Azure practitioner, helping businesses modernize their operations through cloud infrastructure and intelligent automation.
